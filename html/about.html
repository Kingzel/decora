about us

Our Story

Printing Equipment & Materials (Private) Ltd was established in 1995. We are a partner in the packaging industry for Cutting and Creasing solutions for boxboard and corrugated board. We provide TOP QUALITY Tools & Accessories of world renowned brands for our customers who are striving for excellence in quality and productivity. Our Product portfolio offer a broad choice for Die Makers, Die Cutters & Printers covering Box line, Form line, Print line and Process line.

Our Founder

R J C WIJESEKERA Founder, Chairman & Managing Director of the Company. Possess training on Printing Technology at the London College of Printing. A professional printer with over 50 years experience in the state sector and in a leading mercantile company in Sri Lanka. He has undergone intensive training in Die Cutting Technology at CITO SYSTEMS GmBH Germany. He is engaged in disemilating his expert knowledge in the production of Packaging Material. He endeavors to keep himself abreast of the advancements taking place in packaging technology in the developed countries and disseminates them to the local packaging industry and promotes achievement of superior quality and higher productivity . He was awarded the most influential leader in the packaging industry at the Sri Lanka Packaging leadership Awards 2019 by the World Quality Congress.
